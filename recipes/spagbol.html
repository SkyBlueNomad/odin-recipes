Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

My mother's interpretation of an Italian classic, surely taken from a cookbook through the years, although I'm not sure when or where.

Quick and easy, although a longer cooking time allows the flavours to really develop.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Minced Beef/Pork
  • Onion
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Carrots (diced, optional)
  • Tomato purée
  • Dried Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Red Wine
  • Beef stock

Steps

  1. Finely Dice the onion
  2. Add onion to a saucepan with olive oil
  3. When beginning to brown, add minced beef/pork
  4. When meat is beginning to brown, add the tomatoes and tomato purée
  5. Add the beef stock and red wine
  6. Add the carrots and dried basil and bring to a boil
  7. In another saucepan cook the spaghetti following the instructions on the packet
  8. Serve the Bolognese on a nest of spaghetti with parmesan added to taste.
